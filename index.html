About RideShare

RideShare is a community-driven carpooling platform designed to make commuting more affordable, sustainable, and social. Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion and carbon emissions while helping people save money and build connections.

Founded in 2023, RideShare has helped thousands of commuters find reliable carpool matches for their daily journeys.
